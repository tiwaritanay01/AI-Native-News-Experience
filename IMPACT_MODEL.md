# Aureum Impact Model: Narrative Value Analysis 📈

The **Aureum Impact Model** is our framework for quantifying the significance of news stories beyond simple clicks, providing investors and analysts with **Information Alpha**.

---

## 1. Strategic Core: The "Alpha" Factor
Aureum provides **Information Alpha**—the competitive advantage gained by translating raw headlines into clustered intelligence faster than traditional news cycles:
*   **Narrative Saturation**: We identify how much a story is dominating the news cycle using **HDBSCAN Clustering** metrics.
*   **Zero-Noise Filtering**: By clustering 25+ articles into a single briefing, we save analysts an estimated ~40 minutes per story node.

---

## 2. Math of the Story Arc: Narrative Momentum
The **Story Arc Tracker** is more than just a timeline; it's a **Momentum-Weighted Vector** ($M_s$) that evaluates the trajectory of a story:
$$M_s = \frac{V_a \times \Delta S}{\Delta t}$$
*   **$V_a$ (Article Volume)**: The raw density of unique reports in a cluster.
*   **$\Delta S$ (Sentiment Volatility)**: The rate of change in tonal swings (Bullish to Bearish).
*   **$\Delta t$ (Time Interval)**: The velocity at which the narrative is expanding.

This formula allows us to show if a story is **"Heating Up"** (Exponential Arc) or **"Cooling Down"** (Decaying Arc) in real-time.

---

## 3. Quantitative Impact Radar
Every narrative node is assigned a **Radar Score** across three production-ready dimensions:
1.  **Bullish Factor**: Probability of positive economic or innovative growth ($[0.0, 1.0]$).
2.  **Bearish Factor**: Probability of risk, regulation, or market downturn ($[0.0, 1.0]$).
3.  **Interest Momentum**: The velocity at which the story arc is gaining narrative "mass" (High-Interest probability).
